Our Mission

Leftovers was created to help users reduce their food waste and explore new
recipes. With a quick click of your camera, you can "get cookin'" with any ingredients in your kitchen!

1. Download our App

You can find our app in the Google Play Store for Free!

2. Take a Picture

Open the app and take pictures of any ingredients you have available.

3. Start cooking!

Upload your photos into the app and see all the possible meals you can create.
